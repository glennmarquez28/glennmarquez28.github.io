About Us

LiveWell magazines focus on human health and fitness with an emphasis on living a healthy lifestyle. These magazines usually target individuals who want to prevent lifestyle diseases and medical conditions or who already have them but want to live a healthier lifestyle.

People of any age can be health-conscious, but the majority of your audience will be in the 18-50+ category. They are generally educated. Health-conscious individuals have done their research and know what they believe about good health.
